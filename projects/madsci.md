## About MADSci

MADSci is the spiritual successor to [WEI](/projects/wei), and is intended to provide a modular toolkit with which to assemble self driving labs from any combination of automated instruments, resources, and functionalities the user might need.

It's still very much a work in progress (we're aiming for an Alpha release in the next couple weeks[^1]), but I'm excited to take all the lessons we've learned from working on WEI and apply it to MADSci.

[^1]: As of 2025-01-19
